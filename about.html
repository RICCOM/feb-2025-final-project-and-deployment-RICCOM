Our Story & Mission

Since 2015, Coffee254 has been at the forefront of Kenya's specialty coffee revolution. Our mission is to showcase the exceptional quality of Kenyan coffee while supporting local communities and sustainable farming practices.

We partner directly with small-scale farmers in Kenya's premium coffee-growing regions, ensuring fair trade practices and investing in sustainable farming methods. Through these partnerships, we're helping to preserve traditional farming techniques while introducing innovative, eco-friendly practices.

Our community initiatives include:

  • Farmer education programs
  • Youth barista training
  • Environmental conservation projects
  • Local school support initiatives
